## Personalization Angles to Use Before Sending

- Mention their niche: SEO, paid media, SaaS growth, webinar ops, podcasting, outbound, etc.
- Reference one operational pain point, not “AI” in the abstract.
- Keep first touch short and low-pressure.
- Offer the audit before mentioning pricing.
- Only bring up the $299-499/mo offer after interest is confirmed.
