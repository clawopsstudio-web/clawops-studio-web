# 30-Day Content Calendar — Agency AI Employee

## Objective
Use the next 30 days to:
- validate the offer
- build founder-led authority
- generate audit requests
- identify which channels produce qualified agency conversations

## Platform Prioritization
1. **X/Twitter** — highest frequency, fastest testing loop
2. **LinkedIn** — best platform for authority + qualified B2B conversations
3. **Facebook Groups** — warm community conversations and practical pain discovery
4. **Skool** — selective participation, value-first, high-intent communities

## Content Mix
- **50% Educational**
  - workflows
  - pain points
  - automation opportunities
  - operator lessons
- **30% Promotional**
  - audit offer
  - AI employee offer
  - early adopter CTA
  - case-style ROI breakdowns
- **20% Engagement**
  - polls
  - questions
  - hot takes
  - behind-the-scenes / build-in-public

## Weekly Themes

### Week 1 — Launch + Pain Validation
**Goal:** announce the offer and prove the problem is expensive

Content angles:
- launch announcement
- time-wasting agency workflows
- $299-499/month pricing reframed against hiring cost
- first automation use cases
- build-in-public founder POV

Primary CTA:
- DM for audit
- comment for workflow teardown

### Week 2 — Proof + ROI Education
**Goal:** make the economics undeniable

Content angles:
- before/after workflow breakdowns
- 5-15 hours/week saved examples
- hiring vs AI employee math
- operational mistakes agencies keep making
- what should never be manual anymore

Primary CTA:
- book free AI assessment
- ask for ROI breakdown

### Week 3 — Objection Handling + Market Education
**Goal:** answer the doubts blocking conversion

Content angles:
- "Will this replace my team?"
- "What workflows can AI actually own?"
- "What if my agency is small?"
- "What if my clients need a human touch?"
- founder takes on where AI fails vs where it wins

Primary CTA:
- reply with your objection
- DM to review one workflow

### Week 4 — Conversion + Social Proof + Iteration
**Goal:** turn interest into calls and close signal gaps

Content angles:
- 1-week audit offer
- anonymized early wins
- behind-the-scenes build notes
- lessons from agency conversations
- limited early adopter slots

Primary CTA:
- claim audit
- book call
- join pilot / early adopter batch

## Weekly Publishing Rhythm
### X/Twitter
- 2 posts/day minimum
- 1 thread/week
- 2-3 reply sprints/week to agency operators

### LinkedIn
- 5 posts/week
- 1 longer-form founder/operator post each week
- 1 comment engagement sprint after each post

### Facebook Groups
- 3 posts/week
- 2 comment-first engagement sessions/week
- prioritize groups where agency owners actually reply

### Skool
- 2-3 value-first posts/week
- focus on native discussion, not direct pitching

## Repurposing System
Every strong post should create 2-3 derivatives:
- X short post → LinkedIn expanded version
- LinkedIn post → FB Group discussion prompt
- Thread → carousel/image idea
- Comment with strong reaction → next day's post angle

## Performance Review Cadence
Review every 7 days:
- top 5 posts by qualified engagement
- number of DMs generated
- number of audit requests
- number of calls booked
- platform with strongest buyer conversations

## Recommended Operating Rule
Double down on posts that generate:
- comments from agency owners
- DMs asking for help
- objections you can answer publicly

Ignore posts that only generate broad vanity reach without buyer intent.
